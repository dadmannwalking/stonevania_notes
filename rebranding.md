# 🧭 Core Identity
> **Identity Statement.** We are a semi-RP, content-driven SMP focused on collaborative engagement through optional server-wide story arcs that bring players together while still maintaining individual game progression at your own pace.
# 👥 Membership Expectations
> **Activity and Content Expectations.** We are creating something that both creators and viewers can rely on. Regular activity and content is expected on and from this SMP from anyone and everyone involved. What "regular activity" looks like may look differently for everyone, but some typical guidelines include **1-2 sessions per week** and **1 video per month**. 
#### Open Questions
- Who tracks and decides when someone isn't fulfilling the expectations agreed upon? 
- What should happen if someone isn't fulfilling these expectations? What does that process look like and who handles it?
- How do people get into the SMP and what does the process look like from end-to-end?
- Who decides who gets in and who doesn't?

👉 This avoids:
- ghost players
- resentment toward inactive members
- “why am I the only one doing stuff?” energy
# ⚖️ Authority & Decision-Making
> **We All Have Authority.** We are all working together to make this SMP a safe and cohesive space for all creators and viewers involved. As the creators, we are all responsible for decision-making for the SMP and major decisions (i.e., when to update or when to advance a server-wide story arc) are usually voted on during a meeting or via poll after said meeting. If a decision needs to be elevated beyond general consensus, it should reside with the admins.
# 📜 Rules of Engagement
> "We  are adults until we prove we aren't." 
> —dadmannwalking

> **Griefing.** Griefing, otherwise known as directly causing loss of time for another person through in-game action (i.e., blowing another player's builds up), is not allowed nor tolerated on this SMP. Such actions (with exceptions (i.e., as part of a roleplay event or when explicitly asked to by the build's owner)), you resign yourself to the consequences which will most likely include banning.

> **Pranks.** Lighthearted pranks and shenanigans are a welcome part of this SMP. Your retaliation is also a welcome part of this SMP. Escalation without restraint is not. If your retaliation to someone hiding your day 1 horse is to nuke their base all the way to bedrock, that is not okay and will not be tolerated.

> **Skullduggery.** Thieving, including "borrowing without permission" is not tolerated on this SMP. IOUs and borrowing are permissible provided that IOUs are respected and permission for borrowed materials is obtained beforehand.

> **Economy.** This SMP typically establishes an economy early into the season. You are free and even encouraged to mess with or topple that economy. Bonus points if it leads to a story arc that could generate content for other creators.

> **Fisticuffs.** PVP is permitted on this SMP. **However,** there are individuals that absolutely do not wish to participate in brawls. This should be respected at all times; if someone says "no" they mean "no" and the brawl should cease.

> **Exploits.** Don't. It doesn't make the game funner for you or for anyone else. Exploiters will be banned upon discovery.

> **Farms.** You are free to create as many farms as your heart desires. You should, however, do your part to make them as lag-friendly as possible. If it is found to be a problem for the server's resources, you may be required to disable and/or remove your farm.

👉 The key is not “more rules”—it’s **shared expectations**.
# 🎭 Conflict Resolution System
> We have developed a clear hierarchy of authority, especially when it comes to disputes and clashes of character on the server. If you cannot manage to resolve disputes amongst one another, the situation should be elevated to a **Mediator**. If the situation still cannot be resolved, it should then be elevated to the **Admin team** who will investigate and make the final call.

# 🎬 Content Direction

With that in mind, we all have our own bases and builds to work on, but we should be actively looking for opportunity to engage with other members of the SMP and work with them to create content that our viewers will find engaging.
#### Open Questions

- Are **server-wide arcs** planned?
